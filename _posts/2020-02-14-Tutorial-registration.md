The full day [tutorial]({{site.baseurl}}/tutorial) on Sunday 14th June 2020 is now open for registration for all ISPRS2020 participants. The reduced fee for the tutorial is 80,00 € for early bird registration before 13th April 2020 and 120,00 € regularly until 1st June 2020. The full fee is 150,00 € for early bird registration and 225,00 € regularly. The number of places is limited so please register as soon as possible.


### How to Register:

The [registration](http://www.isprs2020-nice.com/index.php/participate-submit/#register) for the tutorial on Deep Learning for Satellite Time Series is done via the [conftool platform](https://www.conftool.com/isprs2020/). It is also possible to register while registering for the conference.

After accessing your account on the [conftool platform](https://www.conftool.com/isprs2020/) choose the "Edit Your Participant Registration Details" option.

<div style="text-align:center"><img src="{{site.baseurl}}/assets/img/participationuserdetails.png" width="80%"></div>

The tutorial is located at "Tutorials Full Day - Sunday 14 June 2020". Mark the box for "7. Deep Learning for Time-Series Classification" and press "Save Participant Data" to continue.

<div style="text-align:center"><img src="{{site.baseurl}}/assets/img/tutorials.png" width="80%"></div>

It is also possible to register for the tutorial while the second step "Event and Item Selection" of registering for the conference. The tutorial is also located at “Tutorials Full Day - Sunday 14 June 2020”.

<div style="text-align:center"><img src="{{site.baseurl}}/assets/img/isprsregistrationtut.png" width="80%"></div>

Check out updates on the [ISPRS2020 page]({{site.baseurl}}/isprs2020.html) and [Tutorial page]({{site.baseurl}}/tutorial) as well as the official [ISPRS2020 website](http://www.isprs2020-nice.com/)
